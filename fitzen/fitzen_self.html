PROBLEM

Working professionals fail to keep themselves healthy and fit.

After working for about 4 years in corporate industry, I noticed one trend that a lot of people start developing health related issues and some negligence towards it.


SOLUTION

Seek assistance from an accountable expert.

  • Based on ASTD studies, people who have an accountability partner are 95% more likely to achieve their goals.

  • An expert’s knowledge & inputs are more likely to help you in achieving your fitness goals.

*ASTD(The American Society of Training and Development)


Less commute time for a training session.

  • Cut down the commute time by choosing at-home training.

  • Take online sessions from anywhere in the world.


Cut-down the cost for a training session.

  • Train with your friends in small groups and divide the training cost.

  • Take pocket friendly online training sessions.

PROJECT GOALS

I am creating an app to help people hire a certified and reliable fitness trainer.

Through the app users can hire a trainer for personal training, group training or online training sessions at their home or at the gym so that users who have a very tight schedule or users who have low budget can also hire a trainer and achieve their fitness goals.

USER RESEARCH

I interviewed 4 people who have either a 9-5 job or they are into a business to earn their living.

The intention of the interviews was to understand how they balance their health along with work. Below are the few sample questions which I asked them during the interview.


Sample Questions

  • What is your definition of a healthy/fit person? Do you consider yourself fit?

  • How often do you workout? Do you go to the gym or you prefer home workouts? And why?

  • Do you workout alone or with some friends?

  • How about if someone else is also responsible for your fitness?

  • What factors would you consider before hiring a fitness trainer?

  • What is refraining you from getting a fitness trainer?


Insights from the interview

  • 3 out of 4 users do not work out on a regular basis and says that they do not have time to workout.

  • 2 out of 4 users have gym membership. But due to lack of motivation and time they are not regular to the gym.

  • 2 out of 4 have small gym and a swimming pool in their society.

  • Fear of being judged by others in the gym for the way they look or for doing something incorrectly.

  • Having a partner or an accountable person matters a lot when it comes to being regular in workouts.

  • Trainers should be motivating, experienced and should understand their client’s fitness goals. They should have happy and satisfied clients from the past.

  • Fitness trainers are expensive and users don't know if they can trust the trainers for their fitness goals.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Due to lack of knowledge, time, and motivation in the day-to-day life of a busy working professional

which makes it difficult for them to workout on a daily basis, leading to deterioration of their health and fitness.

HYPOTHESIS STATEMENT

I believe having an accountable expert who has a good understanding of health & fitness

can help users to stay regular in their workouts and stay fit.

COMPETITIVE AUDIT

  • Fitnesstrainer (Direct Competitor)

GOOD

  1. Provides both 1-on-1 training in person and online training.

  2. Certified trainers with reviews from past clients is available on the website.

  3. Training location and travel radius is available for each trainer.

  4. Website is simple and easy to navigate.


BAD

  1. No dedicated app. Only available on mobile and desktop website.

  2. No real time slots availability of trainers. Only general available slots are mentioned.

  3. No information for Group training sessions.

  4. No options to filter out trainers on the basis of their speciality.



  • Superprof (Indirect Competitor)

GOOD

  1. Provides both 1-on-1 training in person and online training.

  2. Special tags given to good trainers/teachers.

  3. No information for Group training sessions.

  4. Option to search for sports specific trainers/teachers.


BAD

  1. No dedicated app. Only available on mobile and desktop website.

  2. Not sure if trainers/teachers certifications are authentic or not.

  3. No information on how much the trainer is willing to travel.

  4. No information on general or real time slot availabilty.



  • Cult Fit (Indirect Competitor)

GOOD

  1. Dedicated app, mobile website and desktop website available.

  2. One stop solution for multiple minor health related issues.

  3. Live online training sessions.

  4. Visually appealing website and app


BAD

  1. No 1-on-1 training available. Only at-center group sessions available.

  2. No information on size of at-center group sessions.

  3. No information on trainers certifications or past clients and their reviews.

  4. At times a user might feel that, too much is going-on on their screen and can feel overwhelmed.

HI-FI USABILITY TEST

After finishing the high fidelity designs, I conducted a moderated usability test with 4 candidates who comes under the category of our user personas. Below are the tasks which i asked them to perform.

TASKS

  1. Hiring sports/workout specific trainer of specific area.

  2. Hiring trainers for at-home and at-gym trainings.

  3. Searching trainers with their names and completing the hiring process.

INSIGHTS

  • Have a separate search page, so that users do not get deviated with the search results and don’t forget to fill in all the details before searching. Made all the fields as mandatory to fill.

    • Earlier Search inputs and search results was on the same page

    Changed to

    Added separate search page with all the fields as mandatory

  • Have a pop up to show the unavailability of the trainer and disable the “Select Package” button for all unavailable trainers. And in search result itself have “Check more time slots” option so that no. of clicks can be reduced to see alternate timings.

    • Unavailability pop-up with “check more time slots” in the end.

    Disabled “Select Package” button for unavailable trainers.

  • Show UI Coachmarks in very first search result so that user knows where to find what information about trainer.
