ONBOARD

No hassle of carrying luggage to the airport, check it in from home!

(CONCEPT PROJECT)


OVERVIEW

As a travel enthusiast, I've been to a lot of places and taken a ton of flights. But no matter how many times I fly, I always feel a little stressed on the day of travel, even if I'm super prepared. This is what inspired me to create a product that can help to reduce the stress experienced by the travelers on the day of their flight.

PROBLEM STATEMENT v1

How might we make air travel less stressful?

SECONDARY RESEARCH

After reading online articles about the various factors that contribute to stress before air travel, I discovered that...

there is no single problem that causes stress, but different individual experiences stress at different stages of the air travel process.

USER SURVEY

I conducted a survey on 34 Indian participants to better understand and narrow down the issues faced by Indian travelers, as the online articles I had previously referenced in my secondary research were from different countries and did not necessarily reflect the situation in India.

Survey Insights:

Stressed users

  • Female Travelers

  • Newly Married Couple

  • Senior Citizens

Travel Partners

  • Female Travelers: Solo

  • Married Couple: Family

  • Senior Citizens: Family

Convenience

  • Most of the users prefer private taxi over public transport.

Most stressful part

  • Reaching to the airport

  • Passing Security

  • Baggage collection

USER INTERVIEW

Based on the findings from user survey, I interviewed 4 users (2 young female, a newly married husband and a senior citizen). Below are few of the questions which I asked them during the interview:


  • Why reaching to the airport is a stressful task according to you?

  • Why do you choose private taxi over public transport? (Where do you think public transport lacks as compared to private taxis?)

  • How far is airport in your city from the place where you live and how much time does it take you to reach the airport? How much does it cost to take the private taxi? Do you feel relaxed after boarding the taxi on time from your home? If not, then why?

  • How air travel with family is different than traveling solo?

  • Why security check and luggage check-in is a stressful task according to you?

  • Did you ever face any major problem in your past travels during security check or luggage check-in at the airport?

Interview Insights:

Stressing factor

  • Fear of getting stuck in the traffic & reaching late to the airport.

  • Managing kids/babies and luggage together at airport.

  • Long queues at security & luggage check-in.

Luggage

  • Difficult to handle luggage in public transport.

  • Extra luggage gets added when traveling with babies/kids

  • Senior citizens often travel with family, that’s why managing luggage is not an issue.

Security Check

  • Lengthy queues and long wait time is the prime reason that makes it stressful.

  • Taking out items from bags during airport security checks can cause inconvenience.

  • Arriving at the airport on time can help to manage the stress associated with security checks.

Private taxi's

  • They are more convenient than public transport when traveling with lots of luggage & other family members

  • Even after boarding the taxi, users continue to feel stress until they arrive at the airport on time.

HYPOTHESIS

If users can handle their luggage easily then they might take faster & direct modes of transportation to the airport like metro trains.

This will reduce their commuting time as well as the stress of getting stuck in traffic and reaching late to the airport.

USER PERSONA

I want to travel with ease and enjoy the journey, not just the destination.

User Story

Ananya is a 26-year-old professional who works in an MNC. She mostly travels solo and finds it difficult to manage her luggage in public transport. This has made her dependent on taxis to commute to the airport. However, even after boarding the taxi, she feels stressed until she reaches the airport on time. She realizes that air travel can be overwhelming, but arriving on time gives her a sense of relief.

Painpoints

  • Difficulty in managing luggage

  • Stress and worry about reaching the airport on time

  • Long queues at airport

Goals

  • To feel relaxed and calm during her travels

  • To find a solution for managing her luggage during her travels

  • To focus on her work and not be bogged down by travel-related stress.

Traveling with a baby is fun, but it comes with a lot of challenges

User Story

The Abrahams are a young couple who recently became parents to a new-born baby. They love to travel together and make memories with their little one. However, traveling with a baby has added an extra layer of difficulty. From the moment they start their journey to the airport, they feel stressed and worried about how they will manage everything on their own.

Painpoints

  • Additional luggage due to the baby

  • Difficulty in managing both the baby and luggage during travel

  • Stress and anxiety during the journey to the airport and at the airport

Goals

  • To have a smooth and stress-free travel experience with their baby

  • To make memories with their baby

  • To be able to manage their luggage and baby with ease while traveling

SOLUTION

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Customer reviews revealed 2 common pain points.

I investigated the websites and reservation procedures of three leading competitors providing a similar service as my proposal. Although their features were largely alike but customer reviews uncovered some common problems experienced by users across all three companies.

Companies cancelling booking just before pickup time

Sudden cancellation cause panic and stress for users as they are then forced to carry their luggage to the airport.

Mishandling of the luggage by the airline or the pickup company

Users may feel that their belongings were not handled with care and it leads to frustration and loss of trust.

Opportunity #1

HMW make sure that no bookings get cancelled?


If the pickup company has sufficient resources and good routing software, then the only reason due to which they would need to cancel a user's booking is if the pickup driver does not have enough time to reach the user's location, collect their bags, and return to the luggage storage warehouse or to the airport for the luggage check-in within the allotted time.


Solution:

Offering additional discount on a specific time slot for a specific pickup area.

This will encourage users of a specific area to choose pickup in specific time slot which will simplify the pickup process for the driver, allowing them to complete pickups in one area before moving to another. This eliminates the need for constant back and forth travel between areas, saving time which means completing all the scheduled bookings and no cancellations!

Opportunity #2

HMW make sure that no luggage is mishandled?


Where is it going wrong?

If the pickup company is solely held accountable for luggage mishandling, then where is it going wrong?


Incorrect labelling

Incorrect labeling of luggage by the pickup agent causing bags to be loaded onto incorrect flights and ending up at unintended destinations.

Lack of infrastructure

For example, in a warehouse with 200 bags, it can be challenging for an individual to determine which bags need to be checked in and at...

For example, in a warehouse with 200 bags, it can be challenging for an individual to determine which bags need to be checked in and at what time, as the flights associated with each bag may have different departure times (e.g. 11:00 am or 5:00 pm). Automation is necessary to manage this task effectively.

Incorrect boarding

While it is highly unlikely, but there is a possibility of an airport agent to check in the luggage to the wrong airline. However, each luggage is linked to a specific airline ticket/ boarding pass, which is checked by the airline staff during check-in, reducing the chance of such errors.


Solution:

Use QR-code enabled Onboard bag tags.


  • Ask users to verify their name and flight details on Onboard bag tags at the time of pickup.

  • Implement the use of QR codes to scan luggage at luggage storage warehouse, allowing the system to access flight information of that particular bag so that it can alert the staff to check in the bags on time.

  • Upload pictures of Airline bag tag and Onboard tag with all the details clearly visible, ensuring their peace of mind that their bags have been checked in to the correct flight.

LOFI SKETCHES

Things I kept in mind before drawing sketches

  • As the concept of checking in luggage from home is relatively new in India and not widely known, so try to explain what company does, their operational procedures, the geographical coverage of service, the benefits it offers to customers, and try to establish trust and reliability among users.

  • During the booking process, allow users to check if the service is available in their area and on their flight or not before anything else.

  • Keep the booking steps similar to the standard flight booking process, as it is already a familiar and user-friendly format for most users.

USABILITY TEST

I conducted a moderated usability test on four participants who fall under the category of our user personas. During the test, I asked them to complete below tasks:

TASKS

  • Check the price and book the pickup service for 2 bags of you and your friend for the flight from Delhi to Leh.

  • Make modifications to the existing luggage pickup reservation for the Delhi to Leh flight, where you need to re-schedule the flight to the one which departs at 10:00 am.

  • Check the final status of your luggage to find out if it's successfully checked in or not?



Major changes:

  1. Moved luggage pickup details to the top of the page

    • To address the users' preference for confirming their luggage pickup details first, the pickup slots, the number of bags scheduled for pickup, and the pickup address have been moved to the top of the page.

    • The user interface (UI) has been updated to enhance its visual appeal and improve visual hierarchy. Different icons have been added to aid users in quickly identify and navigate to the type of data represented by each field.
